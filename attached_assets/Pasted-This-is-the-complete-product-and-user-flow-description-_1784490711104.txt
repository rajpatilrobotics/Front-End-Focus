This is the complete product and user-flow description for ContextFirst Nexus, including the finished TASK-039 direction.
Important: TASK-039 is currently completed in its worktree but not yet integrated into main. Therefore, use the TASK-039 local preview as the latest UI reference for Figma.
1. What ContextFirst Nexus is
ContextFirst Nexus is a source-grounded case-preparation workspace for qualified practitioners working on trafficking-related forced-criminality matters.
It helps a practitioner answer:
What does the case packet actually document about the relationship between alleged conduct and possible coercion, what conflicts or remains unknown, and what needs human review before a safe handoff?
The system organizes and suggests. The practitioner makes every consequential decision.
2. Who uses it
Primary users:
Legal-aid practitioners
Defence lawyers
Public defenders
Court-navigation practitioners
NGO legal practitioners
Authorized supervisors or demo evaluators
The affected person described in the records is not the direct user.
The app is not:
A survivor chatbot
A hotline or emergency service
A reporting channel
A law-enforcement intake system
A legal-advice system
A trafficking-determination system

14. Context gaps
The app presents questions created by missing or conflicting information.
Examples:
Which arrival date is supported?
Is the task log’s provenance known?
Is an interpreter arranged?
Is document custody independently supported?
The practitioner can:
Answer with separately labelled human context
Defer the question
Preserve it as unknown
Mark it outside scope
Unknown is a valid result. It is not converted into an adverse inference.

17. Strongest interaction: evidence withdrawal
The practitioner withdraws CAND-TASK-0402, which previously supported an alleged task-to-coercion relationship.
The system then:
Shows the affected dependencies
Requires a withdrawal reason
Preserves the historical evidence and decision
Marks only reachable dependent Nexus items unresolved
Preserves unrelated decisions
Revokes export readiness
Requires renewed human review
The practitioner then records the required limitation:
Insufficient evidence to support a link between the 2025-04-02 alleged communication and an assigned task.
This demonstrates that the system does not preserve a conclusion after its evidence is removed.
